I study the complex interactions between the environment and society in a rapidly changing world

My research leverages remote sensing and ecological forecasting to better understand how natural systems and human activities intersect, with a focus on public health, environmental justice, and human-wildlife dynamics. I investigate how the exploitation of natural resources increases the vulnerability of ecosystems to future risks. My work is driven by a commitment to conservation, sustainability, and advancing nature-based solutions to solve environmental challenges.
