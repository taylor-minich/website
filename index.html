I research the complex interactions between social and ecological systems in a rapidly changing world

My research leverages remote sensing, geospatial modeling, and ecological forecasting to better understand how natural systems and human activities intersect, with a focus on public health, environmental justice, and human-wildlife dynamics. I investigate how the exploitation of natural resources increases the vulnerability of ecosystems to future risks. My work is driven by a commitment to conservation, sustainability, and advancing nature-based solutions to environmental challenges.
